## About PartHarbor

PartHarbor is a community-driven marketplace that connects people who need spare parts with a global network of makers who can 3D print them. Think of it as a harbor where requests dock and makers help bring replacement parts to life.

### Key Features

- 🔍 **Browse Verified Models** - Access a growing library of community-verified 3D printable spare parts
- 🎯 **Dock Requests** - Can't find what you need? Submit a request with photos and measurements
- 🗺️ **Local Docks** - Connect with nearby makers, FabLabs, and repair cafés to print your parts
- ✅ **Quality Assurance** - Models are verified by the community for fit and function
- 🛠️ **Right to Repair** - Supporting the right to repair movement by making parts accessible
- 📐 **Precise Specs** - Detailed material recommendations and tolerance notes
